**Call Centre Performance & Workforce Optimization Dashboard**

**Project Overview**

Customer support teams handle thousands of customer calls daily. However, without proper analytics, it becomes difficult to monitor operational performance, identify service bottlenecks, and optimize staffing schedules.

This project analyzes 5,000 customer support calls across agents to evaluate call center performance using key operational metrics such as call volume, resolution rate, abandonment rate, average handling time (AHT), and customer satisfaction (CSAT).

The dashboard provides insights into agent productivity, peak call periods, and service efficiency, helping management make data-driven operational decisions.

**Business Problem**

Customer service leadership lacked visibility into operational metrics required to manage call center performance effectively. High customer wait times and call abandonment rates were suspected but not clearly quantified.

The goal of this project was to build a dashboard that provides:

1. Clear visibility into customer support performance
2. Agent-level productivity insights
3. Call volume trends
4. Service quality indicators

**Key Business Questions**

1. What is the total call volume and how efficiently are calls being resolved?
2. What percentage of customers abandon calls before reaching an agent?
3. When do call volumes peak during the week and day?
4. How do agents perform across key service metrics?
5. Are response times impacting customer satisfaction?

**Dashboard Overview**

The Power BI dashboard tracks key operational metrics including:

1. Total Calls
2. Call Resolution Rate
3. Call Abandonment Rate
4. Average Handling Time
5. Average Speed to Answer
6. Customer Satisfaction (CSAT)

**Key Insights**

1️⃣ **High Call Abandonment Rate:** The analysis shows a call abandonment rate of 18.92%, meaning nearly 1 in 5 customers disconnect before reaching an agent.
**Recommendation:** Increase agent availability during peak hours to reduce wait times and improve service accessibility.

2️⃣ Slow Response Time: Average call answer time is 67 seconds, exceeding the typical call center benchmark of 20–30 seconds.
**Recommendation:** Optimize staffing levels during peak periods and improve call routing efficiency.

3️⃣ **Peak Call Days:** Call volumes are highest on Monday and Saturday, indicating higher support demand at the beginning of the week and during peak service periods.
**Recommendation:** Schedule additional agents during these days to maintain service levels.

4️⃣ **Peak Call Hours:** The highest call volumes occur between 11 AM and 1 PM, suggesting concentrated demand during midday.
**Recommendation:** Increase agent coverage during these hours to reduce wait times.

5️⃣ **Agent Performance Variability:** While the overall call resolution rate is ~89.9%, individual agent performance varies in response speed and customer satisfaction scores.
**Recommendation:** Provide targeted training to agents with slower response times to improve service efficiency.
